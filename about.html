About AutoMindBot

Built by students, for students.

Our Mission

AutoMindBot was born out of a simple frustration: students spend too much time on repetitive, mundane tasks and form-filling, instead of actually learning. Our mission is to leverage AI to handle the busywork, boosting your productivity and saving you hours of time every week.

Focus on Students

We understand the pressure of deadlines, assignments, and constant administrative hurdles in education. AutoMindBot is specifically calibrated for student workflows, utilizing realistic user profiles and smart automation to simulate natural, human-like responses.

Productivity & Automation

Through our advanced MindPower Credit System and intuitive UI, we give you a one-click control center to start, pause, or stop automation. Whether it's multi-page forms or bulk submissions, AutoMindBot acts as your personal digital assistant on your Windows PC.
