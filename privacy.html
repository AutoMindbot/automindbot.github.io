Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and maintain your trust.

1. Minimal Data Collection

We believe in privacy by design. We only collect the bare minimum data required to operate your account, process MindPower credit transactions via UPI, and manage your user profiles securely using Firebase Integration.

2. No Selling Data

Your data belongs to you. AutoMindBot will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information, form submissions, or usage data to third parties. We are a software tool, not a data broker.

3. Local Processing Concept

Wherever possible, auto-filling and custom instructions are processed on your local device. Cloud sync is strictly utilized for maintaining your credit balance across devices and securing authentication via Firebase.

4. Secure Usage

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted. Our network detection ensures safe and secure connections before executing automated tasks on your Windows PC.

5. Accountability

As a tool built for students, we ensure that our automation mimics human behavior to respect the terms of the platforms you use, while keeping your account credentials AES-encrypted.
