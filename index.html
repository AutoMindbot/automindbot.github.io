Installation Guide

Step 1: Download the App

Click on the "Download for Windows" button to save the ZIP file to your computer.

Step 2: Extract & Read

Right-click the downloaded ZIP file and select "Extract All". Once extracted, please open and read the "READ_ME_FIRST.txt" file for quick guidelines.

Step 3: Run the Application

Double-click the AutoMindBOT.exe file to start the app.

Note: You might see a "Windows protected your PC" warning from Windows Defender. This is normal for new apps. Just click "More info" and then "Run anyway". We do not collect your personal data and are working with authorities to remove this warning soon.

Step 4: Test & Automate

Log in to your account and instantly get 15 Free Responses to test the bot and ensure it fills your forms correctly.

Once you are completely satisfied with the results, you can easily purchase more MindPower as per your requirement. Enjoy the AutoMind experience!
